Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a layered Italian dish made with pasta, meat or vegetables, cheese, and sauce. The classic version includes layers of pasta, Bolognese sauce, ricotta, and melted mozzarella, baked until golden and bubbly. It's a hearty and comforting meal, often enjoyed at family gatherings or special occasions.
